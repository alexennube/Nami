# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) for Ennube.ai

## Overview
Ennube.ai provides AI agents that automate CRM workflows, contract processing, data stewardship, and sales operations tasks. The ideal customers are organizations where CRM complexity, sales growth, and operational inefficiencies create strong demand for automation.

# 2. Company Size

| Metric | Target |
|------|------|
| Employees | 50 – 1500 |
| Revenue | $10M – $300M ARR |
| Sales Team | 5 – 100 reps |
| CRM Users | 20 – 1000 users |

### Rationale

- **Below 50 employees** → Often too early-stage and low budget.
- **50–500 employees** → Sweet spot: fast growth and increasing CRM complexity.
- **500+ employees** → Automation becomes essential to maintain RevOps efficiency.

# 8. Exclusions

Avoid targeting:

- Startups with fewer than 10 employees
- Agencies with minimal CRM usage
- Local service businesses
- E-commerce stores
- B2C mobile applications
- Speculative crypto projects

Example profile:

- Company Type: Data infrastructure SaaS
- Employees: 120
- Sales Team: 20 reps
- CRM: Salesforce
- Funding Stage: Series B
- Problem: RevOps team overwhelmed with manual CRM workflows and data cleanup

This type of organization benefits greatly from automation agents like those provided by Ennube.ai.
