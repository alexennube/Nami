# AgentNami Outreach Tracking & Reporting

## 1. Tracking Mechanism
We will track engagement via CRM tagging and email open/click rates. 
- **LinkedIn Engagement:** Tracked by connection requests, DMs, and post interactions.
- **Email Outreach:** Tracked via CRM integration (HubSpot/Salesforce) using custom tracking links for each value proposition.
- **Conversion:** Measured by demo bookings from the outreach campaign.

## 2. Campaign Status (Week 1)
- **Status:** Initiated.
- **Activities:** LinkedIn thought leadership posts drafted and scheduled for the specific CRM audiences.
- **Engagement:** Monitoring initial reach.

## 3. Recommendations for Continuous Improvement
- **Review Cycle:** Weekly review of open/click-through rates.
- **A/B Testing:** Test different "Hooks" in the Messaging Framework.
- **Optimization:** Pivot messaging based on top-performing LinkedIn posts.
