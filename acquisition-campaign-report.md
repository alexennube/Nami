# Acquisition Tracking & CRM Campaign Report

## 1. Engagement & Conversion Tracking
To measure the success of the AgentNami outreach campaign, the following metrics will be tracked:

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Outreach Volume:** Total number of contacts reached per CRM segment (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, NetSuite).
- **Reply Rate:** Percentage of recipients who engage with the initial email or LinkedIn message.
- **Conversion Rate (Demo Booked):** Percentage of engaged leads who book a discovery/demo call.
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** Estimated time/resource cost divided by successful demo bookings.

### Tracking Mechanism
- **CRM Tracking:** Utilize a dedicated "AgentNami Campaign" tag within our own CRM to track lead source and status.
- **Link Tracking:** Use unique UTM parameters for all links shared in emails and LinkedIn posts to track source traffic and conversion on the landing page.

---

## 2. Recommendations for Continuous Improvement
- **A/B Testing:** Regularly test different "Hook" variations in the messaging framework to optimize for higher open/reply rates.
- **Content Feedback Loop:** Use feedback from discovery calls to refine the value propositions and address new, emerging pain points.
- **Segment-Specific Nurturing:** Create automated email nurtures tailored to the specific CRM platform (e.g., "5 Ways to Fix Your Salesforce Sprawl" guide).
- **Social Proof Amplification:** Aggressively gather and publish testimonials from current users of specific CRMs to build credibility.
