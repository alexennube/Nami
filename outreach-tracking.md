# AgentNami Outreach Tracking & Metrics

## 1. Tracking Mechanism
We will track engagement and conversions using a CRM-integrated spreadsheet to log:
- Outreach channel (LinkedIn, Email, Forum)
- Target CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, NetSuite)
- Messaging variation (Bridge Approach)
- Engagement level (None, View, Reply, Demo Booked)
- Conversion (Trial/Onboarding)

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Email Open Rate:** Target > 40%
- **Reply Rate:** Target > 5%
- **Demo Conversion Rate:** Target > 2% of total reach
- **Cost per Acquisition (CPA):** Monitor for efficiency.

## 3. Feedback Loop
Weekly review of messaging performance to iterate on the Bridge approach.
