# Multi-Channel Outreach Execution Plan

## 1. LinkedIn Strategy
- **Week 1 (Thought Leadership):** 
    - Post 1 (Salesforce): "Is your Salesforce instance hindering your sales team? Here's how to reduce technical debt without a system overhaul."
    - Post 2 (HubSpot): "Stop the 'swivel-chair' effect. Unify your HubSpot data with the rest of your stack."
    - Post 3 (General): "The hidden costs of CRM silos."

## 2. Cold Email Outreach Strategy
- **Subject Lines:**
    - Salesforce: "Reducing your Salesforce technical debt"
    - HubSpot: "Unifying your HubSpot and external data"
    - Zoho/NetSuite: "Scaling your [CRM] ecosystem"

## 3. Metrics to Track
- **Open Rate:** Target > 30%
- **Click-Through Rate:** Target > 5%
- **Demo Bookings:** Target 5 per week
