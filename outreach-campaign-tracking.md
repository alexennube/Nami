# Outreach Campaign Tracking Mechanism

This document serves as the central tracking repository for the AgentNami multi-channel outreach campaign.

## 1. Metrics to Track
*   **LinkedIn Engagement:** Impressions, profile views, connection requests, and DMs.
*   **Email Performance:** Open rates, click-through rates (CTR), and response rates.
*   **Campaign Conversion:** Number of demo requests booked, number of qualified leads entered into the CRM.

## 2. Tracking Template (CSV Format)
| Date | CRM Platform | Channel | Activity | Status | Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| YYYY-MM-DD | [Platform] | [LinkedIn/Email] | [Action Taken] | [Sent/Engaged/Booked] | [Notes] |

## 3. Review Cadence
*   **Weekly:** Review tracking data and adjust messaging or targeting as needed.
*   **Monthly:** Final report generation for continuous improvement.
