# AgentNami Outreach Campaign Tracking

## Tracking Methodology
We will utilize a CRM-based tracking system (or spreadsheet if no CRM is integrated) to monitor the following metrics across all channels:

1.  **Engagement Rate:** LinkedIn post interactions, email open rates, and link clicks.
2.  **Lead Response Rate:** Number of replies or inquiries generated from direct outreach.
3.  **Discovery Call Conversion:** Number of leads that progress to a 5-minute discovery/demo call.
4.  **Platform Attribution:** Tracking which CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, NetSuite) the prospect is using.

## Reporting & Improvement Loop
- **Weekly Review:** Analyze engagement data vs. the outreach calendar.
- **Messaging Optimization:** Perform A/B testing on LinkedIn hooks and email subject lines based on response rates.
- **Final Report:** At the end of the 4-week sprint, evaluate the acquisition funnel and provide recommendations for scaling the campaign.
